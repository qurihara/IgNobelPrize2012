SpeechJammer (Ig Nobel Prize 2012 Acoustics Prize) with HTML5

This Simulator simulate Acoustics Prize in Ig Nobel Prize 2012 by Web Audio API and Web RTC(getUserMeida()).
Here is an official explanation of the prize. Visit here to see for more Ig Nobel Prize 2012 informations.

ACOUSTICS PRIZE: Kazutaka Kurihara and Koji Tsukada [JAPAN] for creating the SpeechJammer - a machine that disrupts a person's speech, by making them hear their own spoken words at a very slight delay. REFERENCE: "SpeechJammer: A System Utilizing Artificial Speech Disturbance with Delayed Auditory Feedback", Kazutaka Kurihara, Koji Tsukada, arxiv.org/abs/1202.6106. February 28, 2012. ATTENDING THE CEREMONY: Kazutaka Kurihara and Koji Tsukada

How to play with this toy.
Push the "On" button to start simulator. With the right slider you can change delay time.
The prize says 0.2 seconds is the best delay for jamming.
